Texas State Bird and Flower

Bluebonnet and the Northern Mockingbird

The bluebonnet is a treasured flower in the state of Texas with its beautiful charm and vibrant color! It is even said that you can be busted for picking them in the wild...

This litle grey fella is the Northern Mockingbird and has been the grand state bird of Texas since 1927!
