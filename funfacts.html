Fun Facts

What do you not know about the Lone Star State?

Texas is the largest continental state in the U.S. at 268,580 sq. miles!

There is a town for EVERYONE! The largest city is Houston at 2.3 million people and the smallest town is Guerra with a population of 3.

Texas has no state income tax!
